About

Eve is a direct-to-consumer AI-based compression platform which harnesses the deep learning and neural network technologies to not only optimize, but also revolutionize the way people access their files. Envision ubiquitous file-sharing at blazing speeds, which empowers our customers to access their files anytime, anywhere.
Eve is a game-changer for data management in the corporate world. Our scalable B2B enterprise solutions, rooted in the concept of artificially intelligent software, allows any company, from the smallest startup to the largest MNC, to manage and process their data with exponentially increased efficiency.
